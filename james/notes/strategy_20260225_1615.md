# James Note Strategy: The "Technical PM" Gap (Engineer Survival)

## Concept
Most engineers think "If I just code better, I'll get rich." -> **WRONG.**
The market floods with mid-level engineers. The real scarcity is "Engineers who understand Business & Product."
This article targets frustrated engineers who feel stuck at 6-8M JPY.

## Structure
1. **Hook:** "Do you still believe learning Rust/Go will double your salary?" -> Harsh reality check.
2. **The Trap:** The "Senior Engineer" saturation. AI coding tools (Copilot/Cursor) lower the barrier.
3. **The Gap:** What US companies actually pay for. It's not "Lines of Code," it's "Impact on Revenue."
4. **The Solution (PM OS):**
    - Don't stop coding. Use it to *validate* product specs.
    - SQL is not for DB admin; it's for *KPI analysis*.
    - Git is not for version control; it's for *team velocity management*.
5. **Actionable Steps:**
    - Stop asking "How do I build this?"
    - Start asking "Why are we building this?"
    - Tool: Linear (Ticket management as a weapon).
6. **Conclusion:** Be the bridge. That's where the money is.
