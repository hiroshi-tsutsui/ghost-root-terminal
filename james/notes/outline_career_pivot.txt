# James Note: Career Pivot & Tech Survival
Title: 【残酷な現実】日本のエンジニアが「技術力だけ」で年収1000万を超えられない理由（PMという生存戦略）
Target: Young Engineers (2-5 years exp) feeling the ceiling in JP traditional tech.
Pillar: 1 (Career / Survival Strategy)

## Structure
1. **Hook**:
   - "Technology is just a tool."
   - "If you want 10M JPY, stop optimizing your code, start optimizing your leverage."
   - Why do many brilliant JP engineers get paid 5M while mediocre US PMs get 15M?

2. **The "Value Gap"**:
   - JP market pays for "hours" or "stability".
   - US/Global market pays for "outcome" and "leverage".
   - A PM who saves 10 engineers 1 hour each is worth 10x more than the best coder.

3. **My Pivot**:
   - I realized coding was a race to the bottom (outsourcing, AI).
   - I switched to PM, using my "Technical Background" as a weapon.
   - "Technical PM" is the rarest breed. You speak both 'Business' and 'Engineering'.

4. **Actionable Step**:
   - Stop reading O'Reilly books on weekends.
   - Start reading Product Requirement Documents (PRDs) from top companies.
   - Learn SQL not to write queries, but to answer business questions without asking engineers.
   - Learn "Visual Communication" (Figma/CleanShot) to spec out features faster.

5. **Conclusion**:
   - Don't just be a "coder". Be a "Product Builder".
   - The path to 10M is not in `git commit`, it's in `product launch`.
