## Strategy Alignment
- **Pillar:** 1. Career & Survival Strategy (Focus on Mindset Shift)
- **Target Audience:** Mid-level Japanese PMs & Aspirants who conflate "Facilitation" (Chosei) with "Management".
- **Core Message:** "Facilitation is a tool, not a value. Your job is to DECIDE."
- **Why this topic?:** Addressing a common pain point where Japanese PMs feel undervalued or stuck because they focus on being "nice adjusters" rather than "decision makers" (Mini-CEOs). This contrasts sharply with the US/AI startup culture where speed and accountability are everything.

## Key Elements
1. **Hook:** "Adjustment (Facilitation) is not a skill, it's a chore." (Provocative start)
2. **Reality Check:** US AI startups run on autonomy; they don't need a "messenger." They need a "Decider."
3. **Actionable Advice:**
   - Stop offering 3 options without a recommendation.
   - Learn to say "No" to feature creep.
   - Use Data/Logic (clean documentation) to back decisions, not feelings.
4. **Conclusion:** Being a "Decider" might make you temporarily unpopular, but it builds long-term respect and product success (and income).
